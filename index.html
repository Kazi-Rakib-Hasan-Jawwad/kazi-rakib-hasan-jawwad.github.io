About

I am an Integrated Master’s–Ph.D. candidate in Biomedical Science at Kyungpook National University, South Korea. My research focuses on medical image computing, few-shot learning, foundation-model adaptation, and limited-label histopathology image analysis for precision-oncology-oriented diagnosis and prognostic support.

My current doctoral work focuses on few-shot and foundation-model-based histopathology image analysis, including classification and semantic segmentation under limited-label settings.

Research Interests

  • Few-shot and low-label learning for histopathology classification and segmentation
  • Foundation-model adaptation and representation learning for biomedical image analysis
  • Clinically oriented AI for cancer diagnosis, prognosis, biomarker discovery, and decision support
